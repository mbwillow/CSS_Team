About the Aquarium

The Vancouver aquarium opened in 1956. It is located within Stanley Park and open 365 days a year. According to their website, they are "a self-supporting, non-profit association, the Aquarium is dedicated to effecting the conservation of aquatic life through display and interpretation, education, research and direct action." (For more information see Aquarium website).

Sam's Review

I have taken my family to the aquarium several times. I have two kids, 3 years old and 8 months old. My wife and I really enjoy taking the kids here because no matter how old or young you are, there is something for you to see and experience. There so many wonderful exhibits displaying animals from all around the world. The exhibits are very informative and some of them are interactive, which is great for kids.

Probably the biggest highlight for my family is the animal programs (especially the dolphin and beluga whale programs). The animal programs are a great opportuntiy to see the animals in action and to learn more about their species. The staff are well trained and knowledgeable. As well, it is very clear that they care about the animals and seek to provide the greatest care possible for the animals.

I highly recommend visiting the aquarium. The facilities are very nice and well kept. The exhibits are well designed and allow for up close viewing and many exhibits have under water viewing areas (e.g. whales and dolphins). There are food services available inside the aquarium or you can bring your own lunch and enjoy a picnic on the grass in Stanley Park.

In addition to enjoying the experience of the aquarium, you get the added bonus of knowing the proceeds go to furthering research and supporting conservation of the animals at the aquarium and in the wild. As well, the staff are very helpful and friendly and are often found near the exhibits available to provide interesting information about the animals and answer questions. Your time and money are well spent visiting this aquarium.

Here is a list of my favorite programs (in order of preference):

  1. Dolphin show
  2. Beluga whale show
  3. Penguin show
