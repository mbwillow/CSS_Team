Vancouver's Climate

General Feature

Vancouver's mild climate is fairly constant all year around. Temperatures are somewhat similar to the climate in northern California.

  • Raining a lot during Winter and Spring
  • A lot sunlight in Summer
  • Snowing sometime in Winter

Typical Four Seasons

  1. Spring: Spring starts early in Vancouver.
  2. Summer: Vancouverites make the most of the summer months which are from July through to September.
  3. FallJ: Fall or Autumn paints Vancouver a beautiful gold and orange colour.
  4. Winter: Snow normally falls in the surrounding areas
